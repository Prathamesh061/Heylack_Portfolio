Full-Stack Dev

What I do

Front-End Dev

As a front-end developer in the MERN stack, my role is to create engaging user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I leverage the power of React.js to build interactive components and ensure a seamless user experience.

Back-End Dev

As a back-end developer in the MERN stack, my focus is on building the server-side components of the application. I use Node.js and Express.js to handle routing, manage server logic, and interact with the database. MongoDB, along with Mongoose, is employed for efficient data storage and retrieval.

Database Management

In addition to my back-end development skills, I also have experience in database management. I efficiently handle data storage, retrieval, and modeling using MongoDB and Mongoose. With my knowledge in managing databases in the MERN stack, I ensure the smooth functioning and organization of data

Who I am

Full Stack Developer based in INDIA

I am a highly motivated individual with a passion for learning new technologies. I graduated with a B.Sc. degree in 2021 and have recently completed a MERN stack bootcamp, equipping me with the skills to develop modern web applications.

In addition to my expertise in the MERN stack, I possess a solid foundation in core subjects such as operating systems, computer networking, and database system design. My knowledge in these areas allows me to understand the underlying principles and concepts that drive efficient and scalable software solutions.

I also have a strong command over data structures and algorithms, with proficiency in Java as the primary programming language. Additionally, I have familiarity with C and C++, which further expands my range of programming skills.

As a tech enthusiast, I am eager to stay updated with the latest advancements in the industry and continuously enhance my skillset. I am driven by a curiosity to explore new technologies and leverage them to create innovative solutions.
